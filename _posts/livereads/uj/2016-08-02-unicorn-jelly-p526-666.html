A hookfoot person says “Nanu nanu klaatu barata! Qonos batleth pon-far! Tencton darmok jafa kree!” which is… well I can see a bunch of sci-fi references in there, no doubt there are more…

  • Klaatu barata - the first part of the famous phrase “Klaatu barada nikto” from The Day the Earth Stood Still, which was never translated but was important in that it was the words that had to be said to stop the robot Gort’s rampage.
  • Bat’leth - a rather impractical weapon wielded by the Klingons from Star Trek.
  • Pon Farr - also Star Trek; that thing where the Vulcans have to have sex very urgently or else die. A favourite of Trek fic authors.
  • Darmok - a Star Trek episode with a cool linguistic twist.

I don’t know the other words but I bet they’re also sci-fi refs given the density there.
